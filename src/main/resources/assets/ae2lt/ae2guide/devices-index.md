# Overload Devices

Overload Devices are a family of high-end equipment that share a unified **module system**. Each device has its own set of module slots and only accepts modules made for that device — railgun modules won't fit in armor slots and vice versa.

## Available Devices

* [Electromagnetic Railgun](electromagnetic-railgun.md) — A charged energy weapon that draws power from your ME network
* [Celestweave](celestweave.md) — Modular suit with swappable modules for passive abilities and effects

## Shared Concepts

**Module Slots** — Each device has dedicated slots (Core, Compute, Acceleration, Energy, Buffer, Submodule). A module can only go into its matching slot type.

**Overload Budget** — Armor modules consume **overload load** from a fixed budget (determined by the installed Core). If the total load exceeds the budget, the armor locks down temporarily. Plan your module layout carefully.

**Capabilities** — Modules declare what they provide (e.g. speed boost, damage resistance, chain lightning). The device reads these capabilities and applies them automatically.
